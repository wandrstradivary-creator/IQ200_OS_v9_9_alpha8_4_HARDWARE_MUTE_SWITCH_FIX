IQ200 OS v9.7-alpha1 MP3 ENGINE

Added:
- MP3PlayerService based on dr_mp3 from the existing dr_libs dependency.
- MP3 SDManager::Stream input, PCM16 stereo output, I2S playback.
- MP3 progress, VU L/R, health, EOF and SDERR mirroring.
- MP3 participates in stop, handoff and Auto Next.

Supported playback in this build:
- FLAC
- MP3
- WAV PCM16

Recognized but not decoded yet:
- OGG Vorbis
- AAC/M4A
- OPUS
