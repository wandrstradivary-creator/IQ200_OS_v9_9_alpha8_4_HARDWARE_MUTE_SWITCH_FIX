IQ200 OS v9.8-alpha2 WiFi AP Settings

1. Flash and open Serial Monitor at the project baud rate.
2. Run: net ap
3. Connect phone/PC to SSID: IQ200-OS
4. Open: http://192.168.4.1/
5. Open the WiFi tab.
6. Test Refresh and Scan networks.
7. Select or enter an SSID, enter password, press Connect AP+STA.
8. The IQ200-OS AP must remain available while STA connects.
9. Confirm AP address 192.168.4.1 and STA address/RSSI in the WiFi tab.
10. Test Disconnect STA and Reconnect saved.

REST endpoints:
GET  /api/wifi/status
GET  /api/wifi/scan?start=1
POST /api/wifi/connect  (ssid, password, keepAp=1)
POST /api/wifi/disconnect
POST /api/wifi/reconnect
